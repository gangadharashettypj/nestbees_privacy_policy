Long Description: Securio Fleet Boss is a comprehensive fleet management solution tailored for parents or owners seeking real-time tracking and control over their devices. With a user-friendly interface, this application offers a range of functionalities to streamline fleet operations. One of its key features is the ability to track devices with precision, providing accurate location data alongside device and driver information. Users can monitor device parameters such as battery percentage, gyroscopic values, and surrounding alcohol levels, ensuring optimal device performance and safety. The application also facilitates live tracking of delivery boys, displaying their current location, phone battery status, and gyroscopic data. This feature enables efficient coordination and management of delivery tasks, enhancing overall productivity. Securio Fleet Boss allows users to register devices and create orders for delivery boys, seamlessly assigning devices to specific personnel. Moreover, it supports the creation and management of delivery boy IDs for login into the Securio Fleet Agent App, enabling simultaneous tracking of devices and personnel. Users receive essential notifications regarding device status, delivery updates, and any relevant information provided by delivery boys, such as Aadhar card, driving license, and PAN card details. This ensures compliance and transparency throughout the delivery process. Additionally, the application empowers owners or managers to modify delivery boy login details, delete orders, remove delivery boys, and unregister devices as needed. This level of control and flexibility enables efficient fleet management and decision-making. Securio Fleet Boss is a powerful tool that combines tracking, monitoring, and administrative capabilities, making it indispensable for owners overseeing delivery operations within the Securio_Fleet ecosystem.
